Potent Organic Manure Fertilizer

Potent organic manure is a premium quality fertiliser offered by Telluria Crop Science, a leading agricultural solutions company based in Ilol, Himmatnagar, Gujarat. This organic manure is specially formulated to provide essential nutrients to a variety of crops, including potatoes, peanuts, vegetables, and cash crops.

The use of organic fertilisers like Potent is increasingly becoming popular among farmers due to their environmental sustainability and the benefits they offer to crop yields and soil health. Potent organic manure is made from natural ingredients and contains essential micro and macro nutrients that help improve soil structure, enhance water holding capacity, and promote the growth and development of crops.

Potent is made using a blend of organic materials such as cow manure, poultry manure, and vegetable waste that have been composted and processed to create a nutrient-rich fertiliser that is free from any harmful chemicals or synthetic additives. The result is a highly effective organic fertiliser that provides crops with the necessary nutrients for healthy growth and development.

Potent organic manure is best used as a soil conditioner that is applied during the planting or pre-planting phase. It can also be used as a top dressing to provide additional nutrients to growing plants. The application rate of Potent varies depending on the crop and soil conditions, but the recommended dose is 2-3 kg per plant or 500-800 kg per acre. (5 Bags per Bigha or 10 Bags per Acre)

Telluria Crop Science operates in Sabarkantha, Banaskantha, Patan, Mahesana, and Gandhinagar districts of Gujarat. With Potent organic manure, farmers in these areas can improve the quality and quantity of their crop yields, while also contributing to a healthier environment.

In summary, Potent organic manure is a premium quality fertiliser that offers numerous benefits to crops and soil health. Its use can result in healthier and more productive crops, and it is a sustainable and environmentally friendly alternative to synthetic fertilisers. If you are a farmer in Sabarkantha, Banaskantha, Patan, Mahesana, or Gandhinagar, consider using Potent organic manure from Telluria Crop Science to maximise your crop yields and protect the environment.
