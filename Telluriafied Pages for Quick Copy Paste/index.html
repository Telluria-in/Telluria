About Us

Welcome to Telluria Crop Science Pvt Ltd, a pioneering company in the field of organic fertilisers and agri inputs. We are committed to revolutionizing the agriculture sector by offering farmers advanced agriculture techniques and new technology that help them increase their yield and boost their income. Our team of experts works tirelessly to provide farmers with the best prices for our products and services, without compromising on quality.

But that's not all. At Telluria Crop Science, we go beyond just providing organic fertilisers and agri inputs. We also aim to help farmers grow their income by teaching them new ideas for side businesses along with farming. Our goal is to empower farmers to diversify their income sources and create a sustainable livelihood for themselves and their families.


We are located in Ilol, Himmatnagar, Gujarat, where we operate with a mission to support farmers in every possible way. We are committed to providing the highest quality products and services that meet the needs of farmers and help them achieve their goals. Thank you for choosing Telluria Crop Science Pvt Ltd as your trusted partner in organic fertilisers and agriculture.
